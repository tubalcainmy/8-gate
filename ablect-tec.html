Property Authority Required for Solar
Solar installation requires consent from the property owner. Without written landlord permission, our certified installers cannot proceed — this protects both you and the property owner legally.
  • Panels are a permanent structural modification to the roof
  • Installers require documented permission before any work begins
  • Getting landlord permission is often simpler than expected
If you can obtain written permission from your landlord, come back and complete this assessment — many renters successfully install solar once they have it in writing.
